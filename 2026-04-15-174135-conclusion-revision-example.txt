> what is body doubling?                                                        

● Working in the physical or virtual presence of another person while you each
  do your own separate tasks. No helping, no collaborating — just someone else
  being there. It works because the presence of another person tends to reduce
  the friction of starting and staying on task.

  In practice: a friend on a video call who's also doing homework, a library or
  coffee shop where other people are working, or even a livestream of people
  studying. The point is shared presence, not shared work.
